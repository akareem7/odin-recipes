Jack's Famous Mexican Taco's

There's not a human on this planet who doesn't enjoy themselves a couple of tacos and Jack Hislop has been on his mission to perfect his ancient Mexican family recipe for years now. With a combination of good quality steak and his prefered choice of corn tortilla, there's nothing stopping you from being able to make Jack's legendary Mexican tacos!

To start, you'll need

  • Corn or Flour Tortilla
  • Steak
  • Cast Iron Pan
  • White Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Tongs

Now it's time to get cooking!

  1. Finely dice your onions and tomatoes and mix them together in a bowl
  2. Heat your cast iron skillet as hot as possible (it should be smoking)
  3. Place the steak in and cook for 1 minute 30 seconds on each side
  4. Once the crust is set, turn the heat to medium and cook for 1 minute 30 seconds on each side again
  5. Remove the steak and let it rest as you prepare the tortillas
  6. In the same pan, toss your choice of tortilla, corn or flour, and flip every 20 seconds until lightly toasted and warm
  7. Cut your steak into strips and load them into your tortillas
  8. Add your onions and tomatoes on top of your tacos and enjoy Jack's legendary taco recipe!
