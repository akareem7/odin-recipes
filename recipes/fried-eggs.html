Fried Eggs

Eggs are one of the most basic foods imaginable and for many around the world, it is the staple in starting off your day correctly. There are several variations on how to cook an egg but today we're going to show you how to make the classic fried egg. Also reffered to as the sunny side up egg or half fried egg, there are not many people around who wouldn't love to start off or even end off their day with a perfectly cooked fried egg. Although the process may seem simple, we can assure you that even the small details will leave a world of difference from what your friends call a "fried egg".

Here are the things you're going to need:

  • Eggs
  • Nonstick Pan
  • Butter or Oil
  • Spoon
  • Spatchula

Once you have everything ready, it's time to get cooking!
  1. Preheat your stove to med-high heat
  2. Add your fat to the pan (Butter or Oil)
  3. Crack open your eggs on a flat surface and gently let them fall into the pan
  4. Once the white of the eggs start to set, take a spoon and pour the hot fat over the eggs to finish cooking them on top
  5. Shake the pan a little bit to check if the top of the whites are still runny
  6. If the whites are fully set, it's time to take your spatula and drain the eggs of excess fat over the pan
  7. Now, simply time to plate and eat!
