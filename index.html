Fried Eggs

Eggs are one of the most basic foods imaginable and for many around the world, it is the staple in starting off your day correctly. There are several variations on how to cook an egg but today we're going to show you how to make the classic fried egg. Also reffered to as the sunny side up egg or half fried egg, there are not many people around who wouldn't love to start off or even end off their day with a perfectly cooked fried egg. Although the process may seem simple, we can assure you that even the small details will leave a world of difference from what your friends call a fried egg.
